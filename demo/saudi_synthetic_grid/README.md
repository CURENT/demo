# Saudi Synthetic Case


 ### Reference
 M. Alonize, J. Wang, Z. Malkmus and F. F. Li, "Modeling and Validating System Dynamics in Saudi Synthetic Electric Grid," *2024 56th North American Power Symposium (NAPS)*, El Paso, TX, USA, 2024, pp. 1-6, doi: [10.1109/NAPS61145.2024.10741657](https://doi.org/10.1109/NAPS61145.2024.10741657)  
 https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10741657

 Mishal Hajaj Alonize ([GitHub Profile](https://github.com/Mishal2024)) — Dissertation entitled *Assessment of Wind Power Integration into the Synthetic Electric Grid of Saudi Arabia*, University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

 ### How to Use
 **1. Use ANDES to read PSS/E files:**  
 [https://docs.andes.app/en/latest/_examples/ex2.html#load-system-from-pss-e-raw-and-dyr-files](https://docs.andes.app/en/latest/_examples/ex2.html#load-system-from-pss-e-raw-and-dyr-files)

 **2. Involved functions to export ANDES case to .xlsx format:**  
 [https://docs.andes.app/en/latest/_generated/andes.io.xlsx.write.html#andes.io.xlsx.write](https://docs.andes.app/en/latest/_generated/andes.io.xlsx.write.html#andes.io.xlsx.write)

 **3. How to use AGVis:**  
 [https://ltb.readthedocs.io/projects/agvis/en/latest/getting_started/tutorial/docker.html](https://ltb.readthedocs.io/projects/agvis/en/latest/getting_started/tutorial/docker.html)

 ### What to Do
 **Files:**  
 Prepare the `.raw` and `.dyr` files, and provide a converted ANDES `.xlsx`. Also, consider including a screenshot of topology generated by AGVis.


 ### Notes
 - ANDES supports only v32/v33 versions of `.raw` and `.dyr` — manual conversion may be needed.
 - No automatic conversion for wind-turbine-penetrated dynamic case — manual adjustment is required.
